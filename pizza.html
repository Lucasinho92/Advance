Pizza

This is my favourite receipe

List of Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • Pizza sauce
  • Favourite pizza toppings
  • Grated mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 204 degrees celsius
  2. Prepare the crust
  3. Spread pizza sauce on the crust
  4. Add toppings
  5. Add the cheese
  6. Bake the pizza for 20 minutes
